About
Me

I graduated UCLA in 2017 (Go Bruins!) and went on to work in tax automation at PwC, a Big 4 accounting firm. While I already had an interest in programming, the limitations of the software we used inspired me to become a software developer so I can build more freely. Not to mention, accounting was not the way I envisioned helping others; I have bigger dreams.

I learned fullstack web development and built some fun projects along the way (featured below). Today, I'm aiming to sharpen my skillset, expand my learning to other programming languages and stacks, and exploring ways to leverage these skills to make a positive impact on our world. Now those are big dreams.

Projects

Alwayzon

React/Redux, Ruby on Rails
