About
Me

I graduated UCLA in 2017 (Go Bruins!) and went on to work in tax automation at PwC, a Big 4 accounting firm. Not to mention, accounting was not the way I envisioned helping others; I have bigger dreams.

Today, I'm aiming to sharpen my skillset, expand my learning to other programming languages and stacks, and exploring ways to leverage these skills to make a positive impact on our world.
